## Step 1: Prepare the Dough
- **Ingredients**  
  - 2 cups all-purpose flour  
  - 3 large eggs  
  - 1 tsp salt  
  - 1 tbsp olive oil  

1. Make a well in the center of the flour.  
2. Add eggs, salt, and olive oil.  
3. Mix until the dough comes together.  
4. Knead for about 10 minutes until smooth.  
5. Wrap and rest for 30 minutes.

---

## Step 2: Roll & Cut the Pasta
- Use a pasta roller or rolling pin to flatten the dough.  
- Cut into thin fettuccine strips.  
- Sprinkle flour to prevent sticking.  

---

## Step 3: Make the Tomato Basil Sauce
- **Ingredients**  
  - 2 tbsp olive oil  
  - 2 cloves garlic, minced  
  - 3 ripe tomatoes, chopped  
  - 1/2 tsp salt  
  - Fresh basil leaves  

1. SautÃ© garlic in olive oil until fragrant.  
2. Add tomatoes and simmer for 15 minutes.  
3. Stir in salt and chopped basil.
